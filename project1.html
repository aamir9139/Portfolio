Overview

Flare is an emergency smartphone application for people who find themselves abroad in countries that are in a state of conflict. It is meant to be used by people such as humanitarian workers and journalists, allowing them to have a system that is able to monitor their safety and relay their last known location to their embassy in case of an emergency. In addition to this, the phone is able to store any vital medical information that the user would need to let doctors or paramedic know in case they become unconscious.
