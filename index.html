Project: Flare

Flare is an emergency smartphone application for people who find themselves abroad in countries that are in a state of conflict. It is meant to be used by people such as humanitarian workers and journalists, allowing them to have a system that is able to monitor their safety and relay their last known location to their embassy in case of an emergency. In addition to this, the phone is able to store any vital medical information that the user would need to let doctors or paramedic know in case they become unconscious.

Team: Aamir Ali

Roles: UX Designer, UI Designer, Interaction Designer, Copywriter

Length: 7 days

Project: Medimap

This casestudy aimed to address the various of the Medimaps mobile application and to then make suggestions for improvements with hi-fidelity mockups. The main pitfall of the application was in its failure to meet the various stakeholder needs nor that of its userbase. After addressing the issues with the application, new features were proposed to expand the capabilities of their service so that they could expand their userbase and prevent bankruptcy as the company was not generating much profit due to the single-use nature of the application.

Roles: UX Designer, UI Designer, Interaction Designer, Copywriter
